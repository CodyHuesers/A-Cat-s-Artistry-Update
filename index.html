Meet the Artist

Hi! My name is Amy Sylvester.
I'm a multi-media artist based in Lafayette, LA.
I received my BS degree in Chemistry from UL-Lafayette in 2019, and I work full-time in the field.
However, I have been creating art since a young age, and I spend my free-time as a commission-artist.
I love connecting with new people, hearing their stories, and bringing their visions to life through my work.
